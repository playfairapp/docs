Create a Graphite and StatsD Instance

Log in or sign up

If you don't already have an account on Playfair, sign up here. If you do have an account log in here.

Add your credit card

Make sure you add your credit card information during sign up, if you didn't go here to add it.

You get your first 30 days free, starting from the time you create your first instance.

Click create

At the top of your dashboard on Playfair you will see a "Create Instance" button, as soon as you click that you will see your dashboard change to show you the status of your new instance. Your new instance should be ready in about 90 seconds. Then you can start sending some data.
