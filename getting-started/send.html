Sending data to your Instance

I want operational metrics

There are quite a few tools already to help you on your way to getting operational metrics rolling in to your system. Probably one of the most popular of these tools is collectd, collectd is a daemon that runs on your servers and collects any system metrics you want using its full and extendable plugin system. collectd can be configured to send data right to your graphite server with minimal effort.
